Placements

1st Place. 50 gp, a carved wooden trophy, and free lodging at the local inn for 3 days.

2nd Place. 20 gp.

Participation Prize. Any contestant who earns at least 10 Catch Points receives a hearty meal and good ale, or provisions worth 5 sp.

Tiebreaker

If two or more contestants tie for first place, each tied contestant makes one additional fishing check. The contestant with the highest result wins. If the tie remains, the prize is shared equally between them.

Cheating

Before making a fishing check, a contestant can attempt to cheat by hiding a prepared catch, tampering with another line, or deceiving the judges.

The contestant makes either a Dexterity (Sleight of Hand) check or a Charisma (Deception) check against DC 15. On a success, the contestant gains a +5 bonus to that round's fishing check. On a failure, the cheating is discovered and the contestant is disqualified from the tournament.

Fishing Tournament

An Optional Mini-Game for D&D 5e

✧

A fishing tournament is resolved in 3 rounds. Each round represents several minutes of waiting, baiting hooks, watching the water, and struggling with whatever takes the line.

During each round, every contestant makes one Wisdom (Survival) check. The result determines whether the character catches nothing, lands a prize-worthy fish, or pulls up something far less welcome.

At the end of the third round, each contestant totals their Catch Points. The contestant with the highest total wins the tournament.

Stage 1: Choose the Fishing Ground

Stage 2: Make the Fishing Check

If an effect from a catch table calls for a saving throw, the save DC equals the Fishing Ground DC.

Stage 3: Resolve the Catch

Use the appropriate catch table based on the check result. Bad Catch uses a d8. Minor Catch uses a d6. Good Catch and Great Catch use a d8. Master Catch uses a d6.

Stage 4: Determine the Winner

After the third round, total the Catch Points. Highest total wins. Participation prizes go to any contestant with at least 10 Catch Points.

Quick Rules

Three rounds. Choose a fishing ground. Make one Wisdom (Survival) check each round. Resolve the catch category, then roll on the matching catch table. Add Catch Points. Highest total wins.
