Fishing Tournament

A mobile D&D 5e mini-game
Setup

1. Fishing Ground

sets DC + rule

2. Contestants

mods are optional
Surv = Wisdom (Survival). Sleight and Deception are used for cheating. Dex and Str are saving throw modifiers for catch-table effects.

3. Roll Mode

DM friendly

Quick Procedure

Choose the fishing ground. This sets the fishing DC and special rule.
Each contestant makes one Wisdom (Survival) check per round. There are 3 rounds total.
Compare the result to the Fishing Check Results table.
Roll on the appropriate catch table: Bad, Minor, Good, Great, or Master Catch.
Apply Catch Points, damage, penalties, treasure, or special effects.
After 3 rounds, total Catch Points. Highest total wins.
Resolve ties with one additional fishing check. If still tied, split the prize.

Core Rules

Stage 1: Choose the Fishing Ground

Before the contest begins, choose the fishing ground. The chosen location determines the DC of the fishing check and grants a special rule for the rest of the tournament.

Stage 2: Make the Fishing Check

In each round, every contestant makes one Wisdom (Survival) check. If a catch effect calls for a saving throw, the save DC equals the Fishing Ground DC.

Stage 3: Resolve the Catch

Roll on the appropriate catch table. Apply Catch Points, damage, penalties, treasure, or special effects immediately unless the effect says it applies to the next fishing check.

Stage 4: Determine the Winner

After round 3, add each contestant’s Catch Points. Highest total wins. If two or more contestants tie for first, each tied contestant makes one additional fishing check. Highest result wins. If the tie remains, the prize is shared.

Cheating

Before making a fishing check, a contestant may attempt to cheat by hiding a prepared catch, tampering with another line, or deceiving the judges. Roll Dexterity (Sleight of Hand) or Charisma (Deception) against DC 15. Success grants +5 to that round’s fishing check. Failure means disqualification.

Tournament Log

Attempt to Cheat

Roll against DC 15 before the fishing check. Success gives +5 this round. Failure disqualifies the contestant. Absolutely no pressure, except from every suspicious villager with eyes.
