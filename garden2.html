Cultivating Nature's Balance

Hello, I'm the gardener behind Native Haven. My passion lies in transforming ordinary yards into vibrant ecosystems that support local pollinators and wildlife, all while creating a peaceful retreat for you.

I believe that a garden shouldn't just be viewed; it should be experienced. By using native species, we reduce water usage, eliminate the need for harsh chemicals, and bring life back to the soil.

"Gardening with nature, not against it."

What I Do

Services

Ecological Design

Thoughtful planning that matches the right native plants to your specific soil and light conditions, creating a resilient landscape that thrives year-round.

Native Planting

Pollinator gardens, rain gardens, and meadow establishment. I personally select and plant healthy, locally-sourced nursery stock.

Sustainable Care

Fine gardening and maintenance without gas blowers or synthetic pesticides. Hand-pruning, organic soil amendment, and invasive species removal.

Why Go Native?

  • ✦ Support Wildlife: Essential food and shelter for birds, butterflies, and bees.
  • ✦ Save Water: Deep-rooted native plants require far less irrigation once established.
  • ✦ Low Maintenance: Adapted to our local climate, they resist pests and diseases naturally.
