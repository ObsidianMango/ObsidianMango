STEVEWARE:
THE MAN WHO DEBUGGED REALITY

“The night Steve realized reality had a user interface, he was standing in a Wawa parking lot, arguing with God through a broken Bluetooth headset.”

Walk with the left pad or tap the world. Use Observe, Joke, Sketch, and Name to transform Woundforms. You do not win by killing monsters. You win by correctly identifying what is wearing the monster costume.

No libraries. No assets. No downloads. Just one haunted HTML file and a suspiciously meaningful receipt.
