Our Story

1944
Store opens in Little Silver, NJ—family owned from day one.
1960s–1980s
Family grows and the shop becomes a local fixture—hosting countless celebrations.
2012
Bill Kenney takes the helm, expanding hours and bringing in exciting new selections.
Today
A hub for tastings, classes, and friendly, expert advice in the heart of town.
