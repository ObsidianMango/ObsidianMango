The Turn Structure

1. Beginning Phase
• Untap: Turn all your tapped cards upright.
• Upkeep: "At the beginning of your upkeep" effects happen here.
• Draw: Draw a card (First player skips this on turn 1).
2. Main Phase 1
• Play a land (1 per turn).
• Cast Creatures, Sorceries, Enchantments, Artifacts, Planeswalkers.
3. Combat Phase
• Beginning of Combat: Last chance to cast Instants before attackers are declared.
• Declare Attackers: You choose who attacks.
• Declare Blockers: Opponent chooses how to block.
• Damage: Damage is dealt instantly.
4. Main Phase 2
• Same as Main Phase 1. If you didn't play a land earlier, you can play one now.
5. Ending Phase
• End Step: "At end of turn" effects trigger.
• Cleanup: Heal all damage on creatures. Discard down to 7 cards.
